Administrative Offices

Shashan De Kendra | Hubs of Governance

Beyond its rich history, Bathinda serves as a key administrative and governance center for the Malwa region. These institutions are the pillars of the district's public services, development, and civil administration.

District Commissioner's Office

üìç Mini Secretariat Complex, Bathinda

The nerve center of the district's administration. The DC Office, headed by the District Commissioner (DC), oversees all administrative functions, law and order, and implementation of government schemes in Bathinda.

Role of the DC

The DC acts as the District Magistrate, District Collector, and the principal representative of the state government, coordinating all departments for smooth governance.

Public Services

This is the main hub for essential public services, including issuance of certificates, arms licensing, land records (Fard Kendra), and grievance redressal.

Mini Secretariat

The office is housed in the Mini Secretariat, which brings together multiple key government departments under one roof for public convenience.

Public Hours

Generally open for public dealings from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on all working days. Check the official district website for specific timings.

Municipal Corporation Bathinda

üìç Near Fire Brigade, Bathinda

The Municipal Corporation of Bathinda (MCB) is the governing body responsible for the city's urban planning, development, and civic amenities, ensuring a high quality of life for its residents.

Responsibilities

Includes managing sanitation, water supply, street lighting, public parks, birth and death registration, and road maintenance within the city limits.

Citizen Services

The main office for payment of property taxes, water/sewerage bills, and for obtaining trade licenses or building plan approvals.

Key Projects

Oversees major urban development projects, including the city's inclusion in the Smart Cities Mission and initiatives for solid waste management.

Contact

Citizens can register complaints or queries related to civic issues through the official MCB portal or helpline numbers.

District & Sessions Court

üìç District Court Complex, Bathinda

The pillar of justice for the district. This complex houses the District & Sessions Judge and various other judicial courts that handle civil and criminal cases for the Bathinda jurisdiction.

Jurisdiction

Holds judicial authority over all civil and criminal matters arising within the Bathinda district, including subordinate courts in Talwandi Sabo.

Public Access

The complex is open to the public for case hearings, filing petitions, and accessing legal aid services provided by the District Legal Services Authority (DLSA).

Bar Association

The complex also hosts the offices of the District Bar Association, where hundreds of lawyers practice and provide legal counsel to the public.

Significance

Ensures the rule of law is upheld and provides a forum for justice and dispute resolution for all citizens of the district.
