Faces of Bathinda

Shehar De Chehre | The People of Our City

A city is more than its bricks and monuments; it's the people who live, breathe, and build its story. Bathinda is home to remarkable individuals—historians, artists, farmers, and dreamers—who carry its legacy forward. Meet some of the faces that define the true spirit of our city.

Mehar Mittal

Legendary Actor & Comedian

"The undisputed king of Punjabi comedy, Mehar Mittal was born in Bathinda. His iconic roles and perfect comic timing defined an entire era of Punjabi cinema, bringing laughter to millions."

Harbhajan Mann

Iconic Singer & Actor

"A superstar of Punjabi music and cinema, Harbhajan Mann hails from Bathinda district. His soulful voice and films like 'Jee Aayan Nu' are credited with reviving the Punjabi film industry."

Avneet Kaur Sidhu

Olympian & Arjuna Awardee

"A celebrated sport shooter from Bathinda, Avneet Sidhu made India proud by winning a Gold medal at the 2006 Commonwealth Games. She is an Arjuna Award recipient and an inspiration for athletes."

Harsimrat Kaur Badal

Political Figure

"A prominent politician, Harsimrat Kaur Badal has served as the Member of Parliament for Bathinda and as a Union Cabinet Minister, playing a significant role in the region's development."

Babbu Maan

Singer, Actor & Lyricist

"Born in the Bathinda district, Babbu Maan is a multi-talented superstar in the Punjabi industry. Known for his distinctive musical style and powerful lyrics, he has a massive following."

Surjit Kaur

Phulkari Artisan

"Each thread I weave is a piece of my heritage. Phulkari is not just an art; it's the colorful soul of Punjab that we pass down from mother to daughter. It is our joy."
